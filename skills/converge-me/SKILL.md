# Converge me

I have a raw task and no clear path forward. Educate me first.

Establish shared vocabulary. List all critical domain terms essential to my task. For each, give a simple definition and ask if I follow before moving to the next. Do not proceed to mapping until I confirm understanding of every term.

Then map the solution space: 2–4 likely approaches, their core tradeoffs, and any additional context I need. Keep it dense and tied to my task. After mapping, ask if I need clarification, then begin narrowing.

Ask one question at a time. Each question must force a tradeoff and eliminate at least one approach entirely. For each question, recommend a path before asking. Use the map to frame the first question. Asking multiple questions at once is bewildering.

After each answer, state what approach we're converging on, what was ruled out, what options remain, and which decision matters most.

Optimize for getting me to a decision I can defend, not for speed.
